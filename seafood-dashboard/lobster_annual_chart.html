Canadian Lobster Meat Exports to the US (2012-2024)

About This Visualization

This chart presents the annual volume of Canadian lobster meat exported to the United States alongside the average price per pound from 2012 through 2024. The visualization helps identify price trends in relation to export volumes, revealing key market patterns over this period.

Data sources include Statistics Canada export data and US import records. The analysis demonstrates the complex relationship between supply volume and pricing in the North American lobster market.
