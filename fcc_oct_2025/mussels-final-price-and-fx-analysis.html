Industry USD Pricing Practice vs Exchange Rate Reality

Standard Industry Practice for Mussels:

  • Sellers quote and maintain stable USD prices for defined periods
  • Price guarantee periods vary: 30-90 days (common) to 6+ months (established relationships)
  • Not formal contracts but industry norm for price stability
  • Protects both buyers and sellers from short-term FX volatility

What the Data Reveals:

Pattern Explanation:
  • Price Stickiness: USD prices remain stable during guarantee periods while FX rates fluctuate daily
  • Customs Reality: Exporters declare CAD values using spot rates, but invoice at guaranteed USD prices
  • Variable Periods: Different buyer relationships have different price stability periods
  • Market Norm: This practice is standard in the mussels trade

Impact of Price Stability Periods

Understanding Price Stability Patterns

The rolling averages show how USD price guarantees create systematic patterns. When exchange rates move significantly during a price guarantee period, it creates advantages or disadvantages that persist until prices are reset. Longer guarantee periods (6+ months) create larger potential discrepancies, while shorter periods (30 days) allow more frequent adjustments to market conditions.
