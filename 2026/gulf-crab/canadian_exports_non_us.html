Canadian Snow Crab Exports to Non-US Markets, 2015–2025

Annual exports by destination (million lbs) & non-US share of total Canadian exports (right axis)

Source: Global Trade Tracker, HS 030614, Canada as reporter, export flow, 2015–2025. Quantities in million lbs. SE Asia includes Vietnam, Thailand, Indonesia, Philippines (primarily reprocessing for US re-export). 2025 data is partial year (Jan–Oct). Non-US share declined from 23.7% in 2015 to 8.5% in 2025 as the US absorbed an increasing proportion of Canadian production.
