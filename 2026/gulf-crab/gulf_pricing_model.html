The NL commodity price model (R² = 0.86) provides the base wholesale level, driven by import volume, border cost, and exchange rate. The Gulf premium — $0.09 in 2015–19, $0.47 in 2024, $0.93 in 2025 — reflects the scarcity factor as Gulf supply share fell from 55% to 22% of Canadian production. Using $1.25 as a starting point for the 2026 production-season premium, peak-season Gulf 5-8 pricing centers near $10.31–10.52/lb. Premium sensitivity: at $1.00 the central estimate is ~$10.06; at $1.25 → ~$10.31; at $1.50 → ~$10.56. The $10.25 level falls within the model range at the $1.25 starting premium.
