Observations
The model captures the broad trajectory of Gulf pricing using supply volume, raw material cost, and exchange rate as inputs. The largest deviations occur during 2020-2022, when pandemic-driven demand shocks pushed actual prices well above cost-based predictions. Since late 2024, a structural premium shift (tariff front-loading, tighter inventory) has elevated Gulf prices above pre-November 2024 model levels, captured by the Market Shock variable.
Source: Seafood Datasearch. Model: log-log OLS regression on Canadian supply, USD raw material cost, CAD/USD exchange rate, with pandemic and market shock dummies. Urner Barry wholesale price quotes.
