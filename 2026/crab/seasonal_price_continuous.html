Gulf vs Newfoundland 5-8 oz Wholesale Price
Monthly average (USD/lb), January 2024 – March 2026
The Gulf premium widened sharply in early 2025 as Gulf product entered a dry market before NL supply arrived. The premium peaked near $1.50/lb in April 2025, then compressed as NL product became available through the summer. Entering 2026, both origins are at elevated pre-season levels — Gulf at $11.00 and NL at $10.62 — with the premium already at $0.38 before the production season begins. The premium typically widens once Gulf product ships in April.
Source: Seafood Datasearch from Urner Barry COMTELL
