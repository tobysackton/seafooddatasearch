Seasonal Price Pattern: Gulf vs Newfoundland 5-8 oz
Monthly wholesale price (USD/lb), 2024–2025 actual and 2026 YTD
The Gulf premium is widest in April–May when Gulf product enters the market before Newfoundland supply arrives. In 2025, the early-season premium peaked at $1.50/lb before narrowing as NL product became available. The premium persists year-round but compresses in the fall. In 2026, pre-season prices are already elevated — Gulf at $11.00 and NL at $10.50 — reflecting tight inventories and strong forward demand.
Source: Seafood Datasearch from Urner Barry COMTELL
