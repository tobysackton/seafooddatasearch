Japan Crabmeat Imports by Processing Country, 2015–2025
Annual import volume (million lbs, HS 1605); China dominant, Vietnam at record high in 2025
The structural shift from China to Vietnam in crabmeat processing is driven by Japanese buyers diversifying away from China for geopolitical reasons, not price. Vietnam reached a record 71.4M lbs in 2025 while China declined to 184M lbs. This is a medium-term structural trend that is independent of the tariff resolution — Japanese buyers are building permanent alternative supply chains.
Source: Global Trade Tracker, HS 1605, Japan as reporter, import flow, 2015–2025.
