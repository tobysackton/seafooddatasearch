US Snow Crab Imports by Source, 2020–2025
Annual volume by origin (M lbs) · HS 030614, snow crab origins
Canada and Norway have only partially made up for the loss of Russian snow crab. US supply increased in 2025 based on increased production from both countries. In 2026, Norway will be stable but Canada is forecast to decrease, leading to a decrease in overall US supply.
Source: Seafood Datasearch from Global Trade Tracker (HS 030614, filtered to snow crab origins)
