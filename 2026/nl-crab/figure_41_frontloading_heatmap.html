Monthly Import Share by Pack Year
Percent of pack year volume arriving each month, season months only | Higher concentration = stronger demand pull
Front-loading measures how quickly product moves across the border at season opening. When the Apr-Jun share exceeds 70%, it signals strong pull-through demand. PY2023-2024 had the lowest front-loading in 15 years (57% and 53%), coinciding with the price collapse. PY2025 rebounded to 72%, consistent with the demand recovery that supported higher season prices.
Source: Seafood Datasearch from US Customs
