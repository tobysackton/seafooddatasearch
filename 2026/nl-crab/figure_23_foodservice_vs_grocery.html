Figure 23: Food Away From Home vs Grocery Store Sales Growth, 2019–2025

Indexed sales growth (2019 = 100). Foodservice collapsed during the pandemic lockdowns but recovered faster than grocery. By 2025 foodservice spending has grown 56% above pre-pandemic levels — outpacing grocery store growth by 17 percentage points. This matters for snow crab: crab dollars are split between these two channels, and the two channels are now moving in opposite directions.
