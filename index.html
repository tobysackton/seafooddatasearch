Market Intelligence for the Seafood Industry

Seafood Datasearch was founded by John Sackton in 1994 to provide market analysis to individual sectors of the seafood industry with market updates and price forecasts. Since then he has specialized in annual reviews of the lobster, crab, and other industries in Atlantic Canada with emphasis on the US market, but including Europe, China, and Japan. This website provides data support and interactive charts for the industry.
